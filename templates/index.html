About This Hub

Strengthening Our Community

This resource hub is maintained by and for our community. Here's how you can make the most of it.

The North Alabama Community Resource Hub connects residents of Madison, Huntsville, Decatur, and surrounding communities with vital local resources they need to thrive.

Our directory includes nonprofits, support services, food assistance, housing programs, healthcare resources, educational opportunities, and community events throughout the Tennessee Valley region.

This hub is maintained for the benefit of our community. If you know of a resource that should be included, please use the submission form above. Together, we can ensure everyone in Northern Alabama has access to the support they need.
