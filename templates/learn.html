Our Story

Why We Started This Hub

We created the Northern Alabama Community Resource Hub because we believe every resident deserves easy access to the support services, nonprofits, and community programs that make our region stronger.

Growing up in Madison, we saw firsthand how difficult it can be to find the right resources when you need them most. Whether it's food assistance, educational programs, healthcare services, or community events - the information was scattered across dozens of websites and social media pages.

Our mission is simple: connect Northern Alabama residents with the organizations and services that can help them thrive. By bringing everything together in one searchable, community-driven directory, we hope to make it easier for everyone to find the support they need.

- The North Alabama Community Resource Hub Team
